Digital products are non-refundable once generation begins. Because each book is uniquely generated using AI computing resources at the moment of purchase, we are unable to offer refunds after the book creation process has started.

⚠️ No refunds after generation: Once you click "Pay Securely" and the AI begins generating your personalized storybook, the order is considered fulfilled and is not eligible for a refund.

When you may be eligible for a refund:

Privacy Policy

Your child's photo and personal data are handled with the utmost care. We collect only what is necessary to create your personalized book.

What we collect:

  • Child's first name, age, and gender (for story personalization)
  • Your email address (to deliver the finished book)
  • Your child's photo (to generate a consistent AI character)
  • Story direction / theme preference

📷 Photo data policy: Your child's photo is used exclusively to generate the AI character for your book. Photo data is deleted from our servers after book creation is complete. We do not store, share, sell, or use photos for any other purpose.

How we use your data:

  • To generate your personalized storybook using OpenAI's image and language models
  • To send the finished book to your email address
  • To process your payment securely via Stripe (we never see or store card details)

Third-party services: We use OpenAI for AI generation, Stripe for payment processing, and Resend for email delivery. Each operates under their own privacy policies. We do not sell or share your data with any other third parties.

Data retention: Book metadata (title, child name, story text) may be retained for up to 90 days for customer support purposes. Photos and base64 image data are deleted after generation is complete.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing books@lifebooks.online.
