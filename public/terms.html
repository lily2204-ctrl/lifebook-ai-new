Refund Policy

⚠️

No refunds once book generation begins. Because each book is uniquely generated for your child using AI, the creation process starts immediately after your order and cannot be reversed.

Each personalized storybook is created on demand — the story is written, the illustrations are generated, and the PDF is assembled specifically for your child's name, photo, and story idea. This process consumes significant AI compute resources the moment your order is placed.

For this reason, all sales are final once book generation has started, which happens immediately after a successful payment is processed.

Exceptions we'll always honor:

  • Your payment was charged but no book was created (technical failure on our end)
  • You were charged more than once for the same book
  • The book was generated but is completely unreadable or blank

If any of the above applies, email us at onlinelifebooks@gmail.com within 7 days of your purchase with your order details and we will make it right promptly.

Privacy Policy

We take the privacy of your family seriously. Here is exactly what we collect, how we use it, and when it is deleted.

What we collect

  • The child's photo you upload for the book
  • The child's first name, age, and gender (used only for the story)
  • Your email address (to send you the book and receipt)
  • Payment information — processed securely by LemonSqueezy; we never see or store your card details

Photos are deleted after book creation

🗑️

The photo you upload is used exclusively to generate your child's character for the storybook illustrations. Once your book has been created, your photo is permanently deleted from our servers. We do not keep, share, sell, or use it for any other purpose.

How we use your data

  • Your email is used to deliver your book and receipt — nothing else
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising or marketing without your consent
  • Story content and child details are used only to generate your specific book

Third-party services

We use the following services to operate Lifebook. Each has their own privacy policy:

  • OpenAI — AI story and image generation. Input data (photo, story details) is sent to OpenAI solely to produce your book.
  • LemonSqueezy — Payment processing. Your card and billing details go directly to LemonSqueezy; we never see them.
  • Resend — Email delivery. Used only to send you your book and order confirmation.
  • Supabase — Secure cloud database and file storage, hosted in the EU/US.

Your rights

  • You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us
  • You may request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • You may opt out of any future email communications at any time

For any privacy-related requests, email onlinelifebooks@gmail.com and we will respond within 5 business days.
